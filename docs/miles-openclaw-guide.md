## Overview

We have built a custom AI agent infrastructure using **OpenClaw** to handle the high-volume research, content production, and administrative triage for Devstars and LWDA. This system allows us to maintain a "10-80-10" model: 10% human direction, 80% AI production, and 10% human review/QA.

The agents live in our Slack workspace and can be interacted with directly.

## How to Interact with Agents on Slack

All agents are active in the **#nuclaw** channel. To maintain order and prevent multiple agents from replying at once, we have a strict rule:

**Agents will ONLY respond if you @mention them specifically.**

*   **To get research on a company:** "@Scout, can you run a quick digital audit on [Company Name]?"
*   **To check a project status:** "@Tilda, is the Salisbury campaign on track for Friday?"
*   **To draft a post or article:** "@Marty, I need a LinkedIn snippet based on our latest GEO research."
*   **For general help/admin:** "@Elle, can you find the latest SafeSmart report from Bibi?"
