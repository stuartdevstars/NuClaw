## Strategic Framework: GEO

### Core Strategy: Bifurcated Search Model

**Research Phase (Top-of-Funnel)**
- AI tool optimisation (ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Perplexity, Gemini)
- Third-party citation building
- E-E-A-T signal amplification
- Multi-platform presence building

**Purchase Phase (Bottom-of-Funnel)**
- High-intent keyword optimisation
- Conversion-focused content
- Brand search optimisation
- Direct navigation traffic

### The "Jaw Effect" Reality
- Rising impressions, declining clicks = the new normal
- 59% of Google searches now end without clicks
- Traditional SEO is declining. GEO is rising.
- First-mover advantage window: 12–18 months before larger competitors adapt
- Most agencies are 12–18 months behind this transition

### Multi-Platform Strategy (Priority Order)
1. Google AI Overviews & traditional search
2. YouTube (3B+ daily searches)
3. Instagram (6.5B searches)
4. LinkedIn (professional authority)
5. Reddit (community engagement)
6. TikTok/Reels (short-form discovery)

### Platform Distribution Targets
No single traffic source should exceed 30% of total.
- Google Organic: 30%
- YouTube: 20%
- AI Recommendations: 15%
- Social Platforms: 15%
- LinkedIn: 10%
- Direct/Email: 10%
